About Backup Parts

Backup Parts is a B2B supplier focused on standard and custom O-rings, sealing components, and rubber parts for industrial maintenance, OEM manufacturing, and distribution markets. We combine manufacturing capability with export-ready service to simplify global sourcing.

Tip: For fastest quotation, please prepare size (ID × CS), quantity, material or medium, temperature range, and target delivery date.

Who We Are

We work with production partners specialized in rubber sealing products and support international buyers with clear communication, stable supply, and practical technical guidance.

Our team understands the difference between “a size that fits” and “a seal that lasts” — medium, temperature, pressure, and environment matter.
