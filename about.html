About Backup Parts

Backup Parts is a global supplier and manufacturer specializing in standard and custom O-rings, sealing components, and industrial rubber parts. We support industrial maintenance, OEM manufacturing, and aftermarket replacement markets with stable supply, clear specifications, and export-ready service.

For fastest quotation: size (ID × CS), quantity, material/medium, temperature range, target delivery date.

We operate at the intersection of manufacturing and international trade. This allows us to understand both the technical side of sealing products and the real needs of global buyers — including quality stability, lead time, documentation, and packaging requirements.

Our team works closely with production partners to ensure every order meets dimensional, material, and application requirements.
