About Backup Parts

Backup Parts is a global supplier and manufacturer specializing in standard and custom O-rings, sealing components, and industrial rubber parts. With experience in both production and export service, we support maintenance teams, OEM manufacturers, distributors, e-commerce sellers, and international buyers.

Our Identity

We combine factory-level capabilities with export-ready services and documentation. This means we understand technical specifications, material requirements, and global supply needs, and can support customers from inquiry to delivery with clarity and reliability.

What We Provide

  • Manufacturing & Supply: Standard and custom O-rings in metric and inch sizes.
  • Technical Support: Material selection, tolerance guidance, and application consultation.
  • Export Services: Export-ready packing, documentation, and compliance support.
  • Stable Supply: Repeat procurement support and supply chain reliability.

Our Customers

Backup Parts serves a broad range of customers including:

  • Industrial maintenance and repair teams
  • OEM equipment manufacturers
  • Spare parts distributors
  • E-commerce and aftermarket sellers
  • Global procurement teams
