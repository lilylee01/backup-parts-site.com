Material Quick Guide

Selection depends on medium, temperature, and environment.

NBR Oil resistance, general industrial use -30°C ~ +100°C
FKM High temperature, fuel, many chemicals -20°C ~ +200°C
EPDM Water, steam, weather resistance -40°C ~ +120°C
